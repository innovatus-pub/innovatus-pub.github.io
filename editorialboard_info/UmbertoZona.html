Umberto Zona, PhD

Member of the Innovatus Editorial Board

Notable Assignments

  • Research Fellow at the Department of Education Sciences of Roma Tre University
  • Subject Expert for the discipline “Inclusive Didactics”, Department of Education Sciences of Roma Tre University
  • Annual research fellowship "Virtual assistants as cultural constructs. Machine education as a challenge for the inclusive society" at the Department of Educational Sciences of the Faculty of Education, Roma Tre University
  • Annual research fellowship "Educating machines in the inclusive society. Limits and problems of machine learning. The case of virtual assistants"
  • Member of the Scientific Committee of the editorial series Education and Artificial Intelligence, Roma Tre University
