The journal accepts original scholarly works in the areas of Information Technology, Computer Science, Information System, Computer Engineering, and allied disciplines.

AIM AND SCOPE

In the face of new challenges in business, education, and the community, the critical importance of new and innovative ideas and solutions have increased dramatically. Such solutions may focus on the role that machine learning, deep learning, and business intelligence plays in the business community. Where can IoT, and Information Technology solutions be applied in businesses, education and communities? Innovatus, the Information Technology Journal, aims to explore the themes and serve as a platform for scientific discourse over the challenges that technology can address and the innovations in that space. Articles submitted must discuss novel unaddressed challenges and how technology can address them, or the study of technologies and techniques in addressing known issues and challenges. Such articles should cover the design, implementation and the findings and results grounded in scientific basis. All submitted articles are subjected to a double-blind peer review process as guided by the editorial team.
